Where our values, philosophy and energy come together.
Purpose driven

selfdriven was founded to help communities self-actuate.  Guided by purpose, supported by community members and rewarded by growth.

We believe authentic growth rarely follows a straight path — it unfolds in chaotic patterns shaped by introspection and experimentation.

Communities thrive when purpose and growth are meaningful.  That’s why we encourage community members to pursue projects that matter to them.

Strong relationships foster deeper collaboration & impact. Whether it’s a facilitator or peer, support plays a crucial role in keeping learners engaged.

Reflection is key to a healthy community. Helping community members begin to make sense of their journey.

Our Core Values

Curiosity

We believe curiosity is a spark for growth. When community members are encouraged to ask questions, explore new perspectives, and follow their interests, growth becomes natural and powerful.

Connection

Activity doesn’t happen in isolation. selfdriven puts relationships at the centre. Communities are strengthened by collaboration, dialogue, and support systems that help people feel seen, heard, and valued.

Reflection

Community is about more than getting stuff done — it’s about understanding. Reflection helps community members share what they've experienced, recognize patterns, and develop self-awareness as a collective. That’s why we embed moments of pause, feedback, and meaning-making into every community project.

Growth

Growth is not a straight line — it’s full of starts, stops, and surprising turns. We value grit, experimentation, and progress over perfection.
